HowMarket Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18/01/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HowMarket ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use the HowMarket mobile application and related services (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

1. Data Controller

HowMarket is the data controller responsible for your personal data.

Contact: Email: [dududaatech@gmail.com] Website: https://howmarket.dududaa.org/

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services.

a. Data You Provide

  • Business name
  • Product and inventory information
  • Sales and expense records
  • Customer details (e.g., name, phone number, email if entered)
  • Staff account details

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information
  • App usage data
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide the Service
  • Consent: For optional features like cloud backup
  • Legitimate interests: To improve performance, security, and reliability
  • Legal obligations: When required by law

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service
  • Store and display your sales, expenses, and inventory
  • Generate invoices and reports
  • Sync and back up your data (if enabled)
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Offline-First Design

HowMarket is an offline-first app. This means:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device
  • The app works without an internet connection
  • No data is transmitted unless you enable cloud backup or sync

6. Cloud Backup & Sync (Optional)

If you enable cloud features:

  • Your data is securely transmitted and stored
  • Encryption is used in transit
  • You can restore your data when switching devices

You can disable cloud sync at any time.

7. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption
  • Secure storage
  • Restricted access
  • Regular security updates

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or users

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed unless legally required otherwise.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (Right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

You may exercise these rights by contacting us.

11. Children’s Privacy

HowMarket is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted in the app and on our website.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy:

Email: [dududaatech@gmail.com] Website: https://howmarket.dududaa.org/
